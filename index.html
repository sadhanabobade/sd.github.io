About Company

Placeholder Fabrication Company is a leading name in Heavy Metal Fabrication and Sheet Metal Fabrication. We specialize in providing high quality CNC Laser Cutting, CNC Bending, Industrial Heavy Fabrication, and Laser Welding services.

Established in 1997, with over 25 years of experience, we are ISO 9001-2015 certified and committed to precision and quality. Our team of over 50 skilled employees ensures timely delivery and exceptional service.

Financially strong with an annual turnover of 5-10 Crore INR, we continuously adopt the latest technologies to meet and exceed client expectations.

Quality Objectives & Policy

We prioritize quality, quantity, and prompt service by utilizing advanced technologies.

Quality Policy

We are committed to manufacturing and supplying defect-free sheet metal assemblies and industrial fabrication products that meet customer requirements in price, service, and volume. This is achieved through defect prevention and continual improvement of our Quality Management System.

Quality Objectives

  • 100% on-time delivery
  • Reduction in waste
  • Proper housekeeping in the premises
  • Minimization of product non-conformances at all stages
  • Increase in customer satisfaction levels
